# A+W Cantor Interfaces & Add-ons

## Interfaces to Third Party Systems and Machines

A+W Cantor provides an integrated solution for virtually all window, door, and shutter manufacturing tasks, yet there are areas where the system makes use of other tools that are connected via interfaces.

- **Financial Accounting Software:** Interfaces to all established accounting systems, such as DATEV, Diamant, Sage, and many more.
- **Design Software:** Integration of LogiKal, KKP and SchüCal.
- **Purchasing/External Web Configuration:** openTRANS® and EDI for automatic order management, and integration of web configurators from adeco, dpi, OBUK, Rodenberg, ROMA, and WAREMA.
- **Systems for the Glass Industry:** Integrated solutions from Lisec and glas online, as well as A+W's own Clarity systems.
- **Delivery Planning Software:** Integration of PTV Mapserver, TRAMPAS, OpenStreetMap, and A+W Logistics Optimizer.

## Interface DMS DocuWare

### Free Yourself from Paper

With a digital document management system (DMS), you can improve the efficiency of everyday workflows at your company. Archive your data so that you are in compliance with GAAP and GDPR. All documents generated by A+W Cantor are written automatically to the A+W Cantor DMS system.

## A+W Cantor Smart Companion

### Scan Smarter with the Smartphone

The A+W Smart Companion app supports your entire value-creation chain and contributes to its digitalization. Scanning via smartphone app is easier than with conventional scanners and cheaper too. The app can also be used as a mobile info terminal.

## Integration with A+W Clarity – Software for Glass

A+W is a full-service provider of software for the manufacture of windows, doors and flat glass. If you, as a window fitter, are your own glass supplier, the integration of A+W Cantor with the A+W production system, A+W Production for glass, is the perfect solution for you. This integration also works beyond company limits, allowing seamless data transfer between window fitters and their glass suppliers.
