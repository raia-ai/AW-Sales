# A+W Software Product Guide

This guide provides a comprehensive overview of the A+W Software product suite.

## ERP Solutions

### A+W Enterprise

- **Target Market:** Large corporations, multi-site operations
- **Purpose:** Complete enterprise-level ERP system with multi-site management capabilities
- **Core Benefits:** Multi-site management, centralized and decentralized networks, fully automatic handling of group-internal orders

### A+W Business

- **Target Market:** Small to medium companies
- **Purpose:** Complete ERP system for small to medium glass manufacturers
- **Core Benefits:** Connection to A+W Production and Logistics, graphic interface for easy use, flexible price calculation

### A+W Business Pro

- **Target Market:** Medium-sized companies
- **Purpose:** Enhanced ERP system with advanced features for medium-sized glass manufacturers
- **Core Benefits:** Enhanced workflow management, advanced reporting capabilities, extended integration options

## Production Systems

### A+W Production

- **Target Market:** Insulated glass, tempered glass, and laminated glass producers
- **Purpose:** Flexible Production Planning System (PPS) for complete rough and detailed scheduling
- **Core Benefits:** Management of production flows, intelligently balanced optimization, individual lite tracing

## Optimization Modules

### A+W iCut

- **Target Market:** Small to medium-sized glass processors
- **Purpose:** Integrated solution for order entry, cutting optimization, and storage management
- **Core Benefits:** Easy order entry, intelligent cut optimization, automatic stock removals

### A+W DynOpt

- **Target Market:** Companies with automatic cutting and sorting lines
- **Purpose:** Dynamic optimization system for controlling automatic cutting and sorting lines
- **Core Benefits:** Improved efficiency of cutting lines, less breakage and damage, continuous glass flow

### A+W Realtime Optimizer

- **Target Market:** Companies requiring high production flexibility
- **Purpose:** Flexible online cut calculator that allows for real-time interventions
- **Core Benefits:** Continuous material flow, dynamic reoptimization of any breakage, online control of cutting tables

### A+W Defect Optimizer

- **Target Market:** Companies focused on quality control and waste reduction
- **Purpose:** Intelligent pre-cutting optimization tool that detects and considers defects before cutting
- **Core Benefits:** Increased quality, significant cost reduction, prevention of processing on defective lites

## Logistics Solutions

### A+W Smart Delivery

- **Target Market:** Companies seeking to digitize their delivery process
- **Purpose:** Digital solution for managing delivery processes with electronic signatures and real-time tracking
- **Core Benefits:** Scale logistics solution, use modern delivery app, accelerate subsequent processes

### A+W Rack Optimizer

- **Target Market:** Companies looking to optimize packaging and transport
- **Purpose:** An efficient tool for organizing and packaging glass on racks
- **Core Benefits:** Reduces labor costs, minimizes risk of glass damage, improves transport efficiency

### A+W Residual Stock Manager

- **Target Market:** Companies aiming to reduce material waste
- **Purpose:** An intelligent software solution for managing leftover glass plates
- **Core Benefits:** No investment in expensive mechanical systems, reduction of manual search efforts, timely reprocessing
