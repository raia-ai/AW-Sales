# A+W Cantor   Complete ERP Solution for Windows & Doors

## Overview

A+W Cantor is the comprehensive ERP software solution specifically designed for window, door, roller shutter, gate, and insect screen manufacturers. It provides intelligent overall organization of production and all commercial processes, from initial customer inquiry through to delivery.

## Target Industry

**Window, Door & Façade Manufacturing**

A+W Cantor addresses the market-specific requirements of companies that manufacture:

* Windows (wood, aluminum, PVC)
* Doors (all materials and configurations)
* Roller shutters
* Gates
* Insect screens
* Façade elements

The software serves companies of all sizes, from small fabricators to large multi-site enterprises, and supports all sales channels including direct sales, dealers, construction business, building supply stores, and internet sales.

## Product Modules

### A+W Cantor ERP

The core ERP system provides comprehensive business management functionality.

#### Quote and Order Processing

All Items in Just One Order

With A+W Cantor, commercial and technical orders are always complete and tested. Whether making windows out of wood, aluminum, or PVC, in standard or custom sizes and configurations, Quote and Order Processing makes it possible to include all materials in just one order.

Key Features:

* Complete order configuration from individual lite to complete element
* Glass and component entry with technical value criteria restrictions
* Comprehensive pricing function with automatic promotions
* Alternative quotes with different materials/series for good-better-best scenarios
* Order changes during production with change order functionality
* Project management module for large commercial/residential projects
* Remake/repair order generation maintaining reference to original

Variant Configurator with Full Flexibility

The unique variant configurator combines the rigid world of industry-specific configurators with the flexible world of a standard ERP system. Define your individual configurator with all requirements and rules that specifically apply to your company, staying flexible even when framework conditions change.

A+W Cantor CAD - Special Design Made Easy

The software features simplified, quick entry with numerous built-in shape templates for standard projects. For special or complex shapes, A+W CAD allows free design of custom shapes while the system automatically performs detailed price calculation.

Global Business, Multilingual Capabilities

A+W Cantor includes multiple languages, enabling you to support customers in their preferred languages. This feature opens up new markets where translation was previously required.

Multi-Site Support

For companies producing in several plants at different locations or managing multi-company structures, A+W Cantor's multi-site and multi-company capabilities allow:

* Combination of commercial/ordering sites, production sites, and logistics sites
* Automatic distribution of products to specific plants during order entry
* Integration of logistics centers into the process chain

Financial Controls

Even before creating an order confirmation, A+W Cantor performs a credit limit check on every order. The system automatically performs billing and communicates directly with the financial accounting department, keeping you informed of payables and receivables in real-time.
